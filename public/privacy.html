Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 24, 2025

Maestro Tutor operates as a client-side interface for the Google Gemini API.

Data Collection and Usage

  • API Key: If you choose to enter a Google AI Studio API key, it is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit your key to our servers.
  • Chat Content: Prompts and media you send are transmitted directly from your device to Google’s servers using the official Google GenAI SDK.
  • Local Storage: The app does not use a developer-hosted backend. Chat history is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently removes this local data.
Important Note on Google's Data Retention:
While uninstalling the app deletes your local history, data sent to the Google Gemini API is subject to Google's data retention policies. If you use a free API key, Google may retain prompts and responses to improve their products. Please do not share sensitive personal information (PII), passwords, or confidential data in your chats.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the Google Gemini API. Your interactions are governed by Google’s Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
