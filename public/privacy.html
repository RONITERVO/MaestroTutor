Privacy Policy

Maestro Tutor operates as a client-side interface for the Google Gemini API.

Data Collection and Usage

  • API Key: If you choose to enter a Google AI Studio API key, it is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit your key to our servers.
  • Chat Content: Prompts and media you send are transmitted directly from your device to Google’s servers using the official Google GenAI SDK.
  • No Backend: The app does not use a developer-hosted backend. Chat history is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes local data.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the Google Gemini API. Your interactions are governed by Google’s terms and privacy policy.

Contact

If you have questions, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository.
